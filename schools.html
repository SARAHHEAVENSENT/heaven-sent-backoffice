If your school serves learners facing hygiene poverty, use this form to apply for support. Our team reviews each application and plans outreach based on urgency, learner numbers, and monthly capacity.

School Signup For Support

Complete the school details below. This goes directly into the Heaven Sent Foundation back office for review.

What We Review

  • School location and community vulnerability
  • Number of learners needing support
  • Urgency of toiletries and menstrual health needs
  • Ability to deliver support consistently and with dignity

Monthly Delivery Focus

We prioritize ongoing support over one-off handovers whenever resources allow.

Menstrual Health Support

Girls can be supported with sanitary products and educational talks.

What Happens Next

After You Apply

Step 1

Your request is logged immediately in the back office for the team to review.

Step 2

We assess urgency, learner reach, stock availability, and possible delivery scheduling.

Step 3

The team contacts your school to confirm details and discuss the support plan.

Step 4

If approved, the school is added into planning for dignity-kit or toiletry distribution.
