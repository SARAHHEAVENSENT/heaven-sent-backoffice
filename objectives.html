Objectives

What We Must Deliver Consistently

Access to Toiletries

Ensure learners have consistent access to basic daily hygiene items.

Dignity and Confidence

Provide clean-care essentials that improve confidence and participation in school.

Menstrual Health Support

Provide sanitary products and education so girls do not miss school.

Health Improvement

Reduce hygiene-related illnesses such as skin and oral health issues.

Crisis Care Response

Provide emergency hygiene kits for vulnerable children with urgent needs.

Monthly Reliability

Deliver monthly support rather than once-off distribution.

Success Indicators

How We Measure Progress

Service Delivery

Products distributed, beneficiaries served, frequency of delivery, and coverage rates.

Qualitative Outcomes

Attendance improvement, confidence feedback, and school/parent health feedback.

Operational Efficiency

Cost per kit, donor acquisition, volunteer hours/retention, and partnership network size.
